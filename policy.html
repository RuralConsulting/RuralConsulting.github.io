Case Study

Environment Canterbury – Soil Conservation and Revegation Programme (SCAR)

Environment Canterbury was given the opportunity to apply for Hill Country Erosion Funding from Central Government in 2019. This was a significant opportunity for the council and rural communities affected by hill country erosion as there had not been a formal erosion mitigation program in Canterbury since the Catchment Board days.

Rural Consulting was asked to help as we have strong connections with the rural community and experience developing extension and environmental projects. We led the engagement with the community on behalf of Environment Canterbury. From this we developed the project outline and aided ECan in writing the funding application.

ECan was successful in their funding bid and has subsequently been delivering the ECan Soil Conservation and Revegation Programme (SCAR).

Given the success of the programme, ECan was invited to reapply to the Hill Country Erosion Programme in 2022 which again Rural Consulting was able to assist with.
